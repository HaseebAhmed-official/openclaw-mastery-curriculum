# Model Answer Packs

## Purpose

These packs help instructors calibrate grading and oral defense standards. They are not meant to be copied directly by students.

## Use rules

- use them as assessor references, not public answer keys
- compare them against the capstone specs and track rubrics
- adapt them to the current release baseline before live teaching
- keep any deployment-specific secrets, identifiers, and screenshots out of the distributed version

## Shared calibration rule

A strong answer does all of the following:

- names its trust boundary clearly
- states assumptions and version baseline
- shows evidence, not only claims
- explains what was rejected and why
- acknowledges residual risk and operating limits
