# Operator Track

## Goal

Produce a confident OpenClaw operator who can set up, run, maintain, and troubleshoot a safe personal or team-bounded assistant deployment.

## Best fit for

- students who want practical mastery fast
- support engineers
- AI operations staff
- power users managing real workflows

## Additional emphasis

- channels and access policies
- sessions and memory hygiene
- Control UI and diagnostics
- release-aware operational hygiene
- remote access basics
- practical troubleshooting

## Extra labs

- channel policy comparison lab
- daily operator runbook lab
- release-note and default-drift check
- incident triage drill

## Capstone

Deploy and defend a production-like personal assistant workflow with safe channels, clear operating rules, and a support runbook.
